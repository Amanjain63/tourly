Daily Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Bali - Seminyak

Arrive at Ngurah Rai International Airport where our representative will greet you. Transfer to your Seminyak hotel. Evening at leisure to explore the famous Seminyak beach and vibrant nightlife.

Day 2: Uluwatu Temple & Kecak Dance

Visit the stunning Uluwatu Temple perched on a cliff overlooking the Indian Ocean. In the evening, witness the mesmerizing Kecak Fire Dance performance at sunset.

Day 3: Ubud Cultural Tour

Explore the cultural heart of Bali. Visit the Sacred Monkey Forest, Tegalalang Rice Terraces, and traditional art markets. Enjoy a Balinese cooking class in the evening.

Day 4: Mount Batur Sunrise Trek

Early morning trek to Mount Batur to witness a spectacular sunrise. Enjoy breakfast with a view of the volcanic landscape. Afternoon relaxation at hot springs.

Day 5: Nusa Penida Island Excursion

Full-day trip to Nusa Penida to visit Kelingking Beach, Angel's Billabong, and Broken Beach. Snorkeling opportunity at Crystal Bay.

Day 6: Free Day & Spa Experience

Day at leisure to explore on your own or relax at your hotel. Included 90-minute traditional Balinese spa treatment in the evening.

Day 7: Departure

Transfer to the airport for your departure flight, with Bali souvenirs and unforgettable memories.
